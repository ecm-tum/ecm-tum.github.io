B.S. Chemical Engineering, Massachusetts Institute of Technology

Jesse's work focuses on solid-state materials for next-generation batteries. This work includes spray pyrolysis, materials characterization, and device-level electrochemical testing. He is interested in automated synthesis of battery materials for rapid materials discovery.
