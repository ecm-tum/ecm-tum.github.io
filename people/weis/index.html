Dr. Andreas Weis Postdoc

andreas.weis[at]tum.de | (+49 89 289) 54 431
Room 46213
Lichtenbergstr. 4, 85748 Garching

B.Sc. Inorganic & Theoretical Chemistry, LMU Munich, 2017

M.Sc. Physical & Theoretical Chemistry, LMU Munich, 2019

PhD Physical Chemistry, LMU Munich, 2023

Andreas’ research is focused on the discovery and low-temperature synthesis of functional materials via solution and solid-state processing approaches. Furthermore, his work includes the development and implementation of high-throughput methods for novel battery materials.
