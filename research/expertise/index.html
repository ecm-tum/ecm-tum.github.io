Areas of Expertise

  • Structure-property relations of functional oxides
  • Engineering of oxide-metal film interfaces, multi-layer thin film structures and strained films
  • Pulsed laser, sputter and wet-chemical deposition of thin films
  • Charge and mass transport
  • Electrochemistry on small and large scale systems
  • Design and microfabrication of electrochemical chip-sized functional devices
  • Memory testing
  • Battery and Fuel cell testing
  • Specialized in micro-electrode probing and testing of thin film structures
  • Cleanroom processing
  • New redox conversion oxides: e.g. solar-fuel conversion
  • Ceramic processing
  • Raman Microscopy
ABOUT
The Electrochemical Materials Laboratory is part of Technical University of Munich's Department of Chemistry. The lab is led by Prof. Dr. Jennifer L.M. Rupp.
