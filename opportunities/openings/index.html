Graduate Students

We are looking for outstanding and highly motivated people to further enrich our group in the fields of solid-state Li-ion batteries, solar-to-fuel conversion, and memristive information storage devices and solid-state gas sensors. If interested, please contact Prof. Rupp at jrupp[at]tum.de.

Undergraduates and Prospective Visiting Students

We are always looking for enthusiastic undergraduate students and visiting master students to join our lab. Experience with ceramic and metal thin film processing, photolithography and microfabrication, or electrochemistry is an advantage. Knowledge on ionic and electronic transport in solid state, fuel cells, resistive switching memories, perovskites, structure-electric property relations, ceramic powder processing, multilayer thin film processing is utmost welcome. For details, please contact Prof. Rupp at jrupp[at]tum.de.

Postdoctoral Researchers

Applications from outstanding postdoctoral researchers in the topics stated above are always welcome. Please contact Prof. Rupp at jrupp[at]tum.de.
